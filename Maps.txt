Bar chart - Comparison between categorical value (length,freq,Proportion)
Pie chart - when a circle is divided into sectors,each representing a proportion of the whole
Histogram - Continuos variable,freq of values bucketed into ranges
Kernel density plot - distribution of continuos variable
Line chart - represent a series of data points connected by a straight line. Visualize data changes over time
Box plot - distribution of data based on five number(min,first quartile,median,third quartile,maximum)
Heat map - two dimensional representation of data where values are represented by colors
word cloud -highlights the most commonly cited words in a text using a quick visualization
